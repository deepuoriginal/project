GPTC PALAKKAD

CAMPUS NAVIGATION

The Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad was established in the year 1980 under the Directorate of Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala, with a noble vision of empowering the people by offering good quality of technical education. The polytechnic college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and offers six diploma programs in engineering and technology.
