The Government Polytechnic College, Palakkad was established in the year 1980 under the Directorate of Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala, with noble vision of empowering the people by offering good quality of technical education. The polytechnic college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and offer six diploma programs in engineering and technology.
